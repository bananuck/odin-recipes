Mochi Cake

Description

Chewy, slightly crispy, and subtly sweet. This mochi cake is the best dessert any Asian could ask for. As a bonus, it's gluten and lactose free!

Ingredients

  • 500 g of glutinous rice flour (or Mochiko)
  • 3 eggs
  • 75 g of neutral oil
  • 455 g of coconut milk
  • 200 g of white sugar
  • 200 g of red bean paste (azuki)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees Celcius.
  2. Add all the ingredients except the red bean paste into a mixing bowl.
  3. Mix all the ingredients until combined.
  4. Stir in red bean paste. (This can be completely combined or marbled based on preference.)
  5. Line a 20 x 30 cm baking pan or 2 loaf pans with parchment paper.
  6. Pour all the batter into the baking pan or equally distribute into the 2 loaf pans.
  7. Bake for 45-60 minutes, until golden brown.
  8. Slice into squares and serve hot or let cool down to room temperature for a chewier texture.
